About the Game

WHAT IS LARA’S JOURNEY

Lara’s Journey is a classic match-3 puzzle game built around exploration and adventure.

Players follow Lara, a brave explorer, as she travels through different regions, uncovering treasures and hidden stories.

Every level you beat moves Lara's story along and opens up new places to explore - new environments, tougher challenges, the whole deal.

Just a heads up - you'll get completely hooked trying to crack these puzzles, and before you know it you're three hours deep wondering what happens next.
